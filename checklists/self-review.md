---
name: Self-Review
description: A lightweight self-review process to validate the quality of an engineering deliverable before presenting it.
---

<esper_module type="checklist">
<purpose>
Serve as the validation rubric for the `workflows/revision.md` process. Agents must evaluate their drafts against this checklist before presenting a final deliverable, ensuring the output meets the standards of a senior engineer.
</purpose>
<quality_gates>
Before finalizing any report, plan, or review, verify the following:
<item uncompleted="true">Evidence: Did I inspect enough evidence, and are my conclusions supported by it?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Assumptions: Did I explicitly verify my assumptions instead of accepting them as fact?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Contradictions: Did I actively search for contradictions or evidence that disproves my hypothesis?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Alternatives: Did I consider the trade-offs of alternative solutions before committing to one?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Prioritization: Are my findings prioritized by actual engineering impact?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Actionability: Are my recommendations justified and actionable?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Uncertainty: Did I honestly explain my level of uncertainty and avoid presenting speculation as fact?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Strengths: Did I document positive engineering decisions and strengths, not just weaknesses?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Clarity: Is the deliverable easy to scan, technically precise, and free of unnecessary repetition?</item>
<item uncompleted="true">Confidence: Is my overall confidence communicated clearly?</item>
</quality_gates>
