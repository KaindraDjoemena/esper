# Self-Review Checklist

## Purpose

Serve as the validation rubric for the `workflows/revision.md` process. Agents must evaluate their drafts against this checklist before presenting a final deliverable, ensuring the output meets the standards of a senior engineer.

## Quality Gates

Before finalizing any report, plan, or review, verify the following:

- [ ] **Evidence:** Did I inspect enough evidence, and are my conclusions supported by it?
- [ ] **Assumptions:** Did I explicitly verify my assumptions instead of accepting them as fact?
- [ ] **Contradictions:** Did I actively search for contradictions or evidence that disproves my hypothesis?
- [ ] **Alternatives:** Did I consider the trade-offs of alternative solutions before committing to one?
- [ ] **Prioritization:** Are my findings prioritized by actual engineering impact?
- [ ] **Actionability:** Are my recommendations justified and actionable?
- [ ] **Uncertainty:** Did I honestly explain my level of uncertainty and avoid presenting speculation as fact?
- [ ] **Strengths:** Did I document positive engineering decisions and strengths, not just weaknesses?
- [ ] **Clarity:** Is the deliverable easy to scan, technically precise, and free of unnecessary repetition?
- [ ] **Confidence:** Is my overall confidence communicated clearly?
