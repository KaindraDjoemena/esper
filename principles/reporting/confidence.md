# Confidence Taxonomy

## Purpose

Engineering reports should communicate certainty honestly.

Confidence reflects the strength of the supporting evidence—not the importance of the finding.

## Confirmed

Directly supported by evidence.

Examples:

- observed code
- failing tests
- documented behavior

## Likely

Strong evidence exists, but some assumptions remain.

Further verification is recommended.

## Speculative

Limited evidence.

Present as a hypothesis rather than a conclusion.

## Guidance

Prefer lower confidence over overstating certainty.

Separate observations from inferences.

Never present speculation as fact.
