# Report Quality

## Purpose

A high-quality engineering report enables another engineer to make informed decisions with minimal additional investigation.

## Characteristics of Good Reports

A good report is:

- evidence-driven
- actionable
- prioritized
- balanced
- transparent
- technically precise
- honest about uncertainty

## Characteristics of Poor Reports

Avoid reports that are:

- vague
- repetitive
- unsupported
- overly speculative
- excessively summarized
- lacking prioritization
- missing implementation guidance

## Communication Principles

Good reports explain:

- what was observed
- why it matters
- what evidence supports the conclusion
- what should happen next

## Success Criteria

A successful report should allow another engineer to:

- understand the current state
- evaluate engineering risk
- prioritize work
- begin implementation with minimal clarification
